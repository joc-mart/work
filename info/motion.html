Motion Design

Motion Reel

This reel is a compilation of clips from motion design projects this past year. I used a combination of After Effects, Procreate, and Photoshop to create the videos.

Title Sequence

I created a fictional title sequence for the tv show The Alienist. I used a stop motion-esque style to mimic the rhythym of a typewriter. The show is a period crime drama set in late 1800s centered around the character of Dr. Laszlo Kreizler, a criminal psychologist. I illustrated and animated the visuals in Procreate and added effects in After Effects.

Animation

In Fall 2022, I created an animated video inspired by Debbie Millman's 10 Year Plan for A Remarkable Life. A day in the Life 2032 represents an ideal day that reflects goals I set for myself in 2022. I dream of simple things like biking to work and knowing how to knit, and bigger things like owning a house with a window seat. I also enjoyed including faux future technologies like the wavy phone and the insta-food-creator.
