Web Design Study

For a Web Development course we were asked to use the atomic design system to study a website of our choice. I created a case study of the chosen website's components and recreated the website homepage using HTML and CSS. The website page I chose to study and recreate was the National Museum of Mexican Art.

Original website on May 4, 2022: https://nationalmuseumofmexicanart.org/

Recreated homepage: https://joc-mart.github.io/casestudy/

Component Case Study

I wrote the components, or building blocks of the website, to match the original homepage as closely as possible.
