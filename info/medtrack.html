MedTrack App

It is difficult for some people to not only remember what medications they are on or prescriptions that they have, but also their function is and when to take them.

MedTracker is an app that keeps track of current prescriptions, reminders, and prescription history to use as a reference when needed.

Process

First, I conducted user research to get an idea of what features users would like to see. I interviewed friends and family who use medications about their experiences.

Then I sketched ideas for the app layout. With the input of peer feedback, I went with most streamlined design that still allowed for features most important to the interviewees.
